Buzz Lightyear

Nearly finished Buzz Lightyear Model. Modeled in Maya. Rendered with Arnold Renderer. Just need to put in the final touches and it'll be ready for rigging!

I've thought about giving this Buzz more metallic textures to make him look less like a toy and more like a real space ranger. Maybe also play around the color schemes and give him a different theme.

Currently having a hard time getting the correct pivot points for the hemlet so that it can properly roll in and out of the suit. It either curves in too near or too far. It needs to slide in perfectly through the gap from where it comes out of the upper torso.

FN Five-seveN handgun

FN Five-seveN handgun. Belgian semi-automatic handgun. Modelled by looking at reference images (no image planes used).

This was a fun and challenging handgun to model, given its slightly curvy design. Most handguns have a blockier symmetrical body like the Glock 19 or the M1911. But this one is slightly more rounded.

F-22 Raptor

F-22 Raptor Jet. This is my absolute favorite fighter aircraft ever since I was 5 years old. Till this day, it continues to be a beast in air superiority.

An all in one package. Excels at speed, agility and stealth (anti-radar technology). Aircrafts usually excel at either one or two of those, given engineering limitations. But not this one. This is a multi-role fighter.

Very common in Hollywood movies too. They feature in most blockbusters that feature any sort of aerial military presence.

Modeled with Maya. Rendered with Mental ray.

A-10 Warthog

The A-10 Warthog! An air support aircraft, designed to aid ground infantry in their battles. It's signature weapon is the GAU-8 Monster of a gattling cannon mounted on its nose. Colloquially known as the '' Brrrrrrt '' Gun, because that's the sound it makes. Brrrrrrrrrrrrrrrt.

It's design ensures stability at low airspeeds. This is vital to air support vehicles because they need to travel slowly over the battlefield to ensure as much Brrrrrrrrrt action as possible. The slower it goes, the more Brrrrrrrrt work it can accomplish in one given flyby.
